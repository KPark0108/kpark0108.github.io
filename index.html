TDEE - Total Daily Energy Expenditure

See your maintainance calorie!

Knowing your TDEE can help you tailor your diet and exercise regimen to meet your specific fitness goals, as well as avoid overeating or undereating. By consuming the right amount of calories for your body and activity level, you can fuel your workouts, promote muscle growth and recovery, and maintain a healthy weight.
